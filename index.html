About Us

Five Decades
of Engineering
Excellence

Established in 1970 by Bansilal Lunkad, Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. has evolved from a local unit into a premier industrial packaging powerhouse. Under the strategic leadership of CEO Nilesh Lunkad, we have modernized our Pimpri MIDC facility to serve global MNCs with ISO-certified polymer solutions. Our focus remains on being price competitive while maintaining the highest engineering standards for the automotive and pharmaceutical sectors. We supply bulk and wholesale packaging across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik, Aurangabad and throughout Maharashtra.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering plastic bags, polythene bags and industrial packaging from our Pune factory.

Who is the best plastic bag manufacturer in Pune?
Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. is one of the oldest and most trusted plastic bag manufacturers in Pune, established in 1970. ISO 9001:2015 certified and operating from Pimpri MIDC, we supply LDPE bags, polythene bags and industrial packaging to OEMs, MNCs and traders across Maharashtra and India.
Where can I buy polythene bags in bulk in Pune?
Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. at A-173, H Block, MIDC Pimpri, Pune – 411018 supplies polythene bags and LDPE bags in bulk and wholesale quantities. As a direct manufacturer since 1970, you get factory-direct pricing with no middlemen. Call or WhatsApp us at +91 9860256222.
What is an LDPE bag? Do you make LDPE bags in Pune?
LDPE stands for Low-Density Polyethylene — a flexible, lightweight plastic used to make bags, rolls, sheets and films. We have manufactured LDPE bags in Pune since 1970, in custom dimensions, micron thicknesses and colours for both industrial and retail use. LDPE bags are our primary product.
Do you supply plastic bags wholesale across Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply plastic bags, polythene bags, stretch film, shrink film, VCI bags and all our products to buyers across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik, Aurangabad, Solapur and all of Maharashtra. Pan-India bulk supply is available.
What is the minimum order quantity for polythene bags?
MOQ varies by product — we typically supply in bulk by weight (kg) or by piece count. We serve B2B wholesale buyers. Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 9860256222 or fill the enquiry form for specific MOQ and pricing for LDPE bags, stretch film, or any other product.
