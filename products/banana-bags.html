Product Overview

About Banana Bags

Banana bags are specialised UV-stabilised LDPE polythene covers designed to protect banana bunches from insects, pesticides and physical damage during the growth and post-harvest stages. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures banana cover bags in Pimpri MIDC, Pune — supplying banana plantations, agri-input dealers and farm produce exporters across Maharashtra and India.

Our banana bags are manufactured from UV-stabilised LDPE that blocks harmful UV radiation while maintaining permeability to allow the fruit to breathe. The bags have perforations for ventilation and are available in blue, white and custom colours. Being agricultural plastic bags with UV resistance built into the film, they offer months of effective fruit protection in open-field conditions.

Covering banana bunches from the bell (flower) stage through to harvest is a proven practice that significantly improves bunch quality, uniformity and marketable yield. Our banana bags are manufactured with UV stabiliser additives that prevent premature degradation in outdoor agricultural conditions, ensuring the bag lasts for the full growth period of 90–120 days.

We supply banana bags in the standard blue LDPE design with ventilation holes, as used across Indian banana cultivation, as well as in custom sizes for different banana varieties. The ventilation holes allow air circulation and moisture escape, preventing fungal growth while maintaining a protective microenvironment around the developing bunch.

FAQ

Common Questions

Why are banana bags blue?
Blue BOPP or LDPE banana bags are the industry standard because blue is not attractive to most harmful insects, while the bag still transmits sufficient diffused light for normal fruit development. The colour also helps workers identify covered bunches in the plantation.
When should banana bags be applied during cultivation?
Banana bags should be applied at the 'pencil stage' — when the last hand of the bunch has opened and the bell is still present. Early covering maximises protection across the full development period of approximately 90–120 days to harvest.
Do you supply banana bags wholesale to farmers in Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply banana bags in wholesale quantities to banana farmers, agricultural cooperatives and agri-input traders across Maharashtra, including the major banana-growing districts of Jalgaon, Solapur, Kolhapur and Nashik.
What UV protection do banana bags provide?
Our banana bags are made from LDPE with UV stabiliser masterbatch incorporated during film extrusion. This provides consistent UV protection throughout the bag's material, helping to protect banana bunches from sunburn, insect damage and pesticide spray drift. The bags are designed for outdoor agricultural use and can withstand months of UV exposure without degrading.
Are banana bags available with ventilation holes?
Yes. Our banana cover bags are available with perforations or micro-ventilation holes to allow air circulation and prevent condensation inside the bag. Standard and custom perforation patterns are available. We also supply solid (unperforated) banana bags. Contact us to discuss your specific agricultural requirement.
