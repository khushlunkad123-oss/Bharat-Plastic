Product Overview

About Stretch Film

Stretch film — also called pallet wrap or stretch wrap — is a highly elastic plastic film used to unitise, secure and protect loads on pallets during storage and transit. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. supplies industrial-grade stretch film rolls from our Pimpri MIDC, Pune facility to manufacturers, warehouses and logistics companies across Maharashtra and India.

Our stretch film is manufactured from LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), which provides excellent cling, puncture resistance and load-holding force. We supply both hand-grade stretch film (for manual wrapping) and machine-grade stretch film (for automated pallet wrapping lines) in standard and custom widths.

Stretch film is widely used in automotive, pharmaceutical, FMCG, engineering and logistics sectors to protect wrapped pallets from dust, moisture and damage during transport. Our stretch film is supplied in bulk rolls at competitive factory-direct prices.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is the difference between hand grade and machine grade stretch film?
Hand grade stretch film is designed for manual application — it is generally thicker and easier to grip. Machine grade stretch film is designed for use on semi-automatic or automatic pallet wrapping machines and operated at higher elongation for efficiency. We supply both grades in bulk from Pune.
What thickness of stretch film should I use for heavy industrial pallets?
For heavy or sharp-edged loads, we recommend 80–100 microns. For standard loads, 50–70 microns is usually sufficient. Contact us to discuss your specific load requirement.
Do you supply stretch film in bulk to warehouses across Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply stretch film rolls in bulk to manufacturers, warehouse operators and logistics companies across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik, Aurangabad and all of Maharashtra.
