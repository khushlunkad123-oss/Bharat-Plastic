Product Overview

About Zip Lock Bags

Zip lock bags — also called zipper bags or press-seal bags — are resealable LDPE polythene bags fitted with an interlocking plastic zipper strip that allows the bag to be opened and closed repeatedly without losing seal integrity. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures zip lock bags at our Pimpri MIDC, Pune factory — supplying bulk orders to industrial, pharmaceutical, retail and laboratory buyers across Maharashtra.

The resealable zip closure makes zip lock bags ideal for applications where contents need to be accessed multiple times while remaining protected between uses. They are widely used for storing hardware samples, electronic components, pharmaceutical samples, food products, jewellery, stationery and laboratory specimens.

We manufacture zip lock bags in a full range of sizes from small jewellery bags to large document and component bags. Thicknesses range from standard 50 or 60 microns for general use to heavy-duty 200+ microns for sharp-edged or heavy contents.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What sizes are available for zip lock bags?
We manufacture zip lock bags in all standard sizes from 1.5 inches x 2 inches up to 18 inches x 24 inches and larger. Custom sizes are produced to order. Contact us with your required dimensions for pricing.
What thickness of zip lock bags should I choose?
50 or 60 microns is standard for general-purpose storage and samples. For heavier contents, sharp-edged items or repeated use, 100–200+ microns is recommended. All our zip lock bags meet the government minimum of 50 microns and carry mandatory statutory printing.
Do you supply zip lock bags in bulk to industries in Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply zip lock bags in bulk to pharmaceutical, engineering, hardware, jewellery and food industries across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik and all of Maharashtra.
