Product Overview

About Jumbo Polybags

Jumbo polybags and covers are heavy-duty, large-format LDPE polythene bags designed to contain, protect and transport oversized industrial products, machinery components, raw materials and bulk goods. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures jumbo polybags at our Pimpri MIDC, Pune factory — producing any custom dimension for industrial buyers across Maharashtra and India.

Standard polythene bags cannot accommodate large industrial products such as machine components, steel structures, rolled goods, large castings and oversized packaging. Jumbo polybags are fabricated specifically to these requirements — welded from LDPE sheeting in custom widths from 50 to 100+ inches, any length and with or without gussets.

We supply jumbo polybags from 50 microns (government minimum) to 250+ microns depending on the weight and nature of the contents. Open-mouth and heat-seal top closures are available. Gusseted configurations are also offered for improved fit around irregular shapes.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is the maximum size of jumbo polybag you can manufacture?
We manufacture jumbo polybags in virtually any dimension at our Pimpri MIDC, Pune factory. There is no fixed maximum — we have produced bags several metres in length and width for large machinery covers and civil applications. Contact us with your exact dimensions for pricing.
What thickness of jumbo polybag is recommended for heavy industrial goods?
For general large goods and machine covers, 100–150 microns is commonly used. For heavier or sharp-edged items, 200–250 microns provides added protection. All bags are manufactured to the government minimum of 50 microns. We advise on the appropriate specification based on your product requirements.
Do you supply jumbo polybags to industrial manufacturers across Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply jumbo polybags in bulk to engineering, manufacturing, construction and export companies across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik and all of Maharashtra. Custom dimensions are our speciality — contact us for a quote.
