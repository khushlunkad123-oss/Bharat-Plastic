Product Overview

About Jumbo Polybags

Jumbo polybags and large polythene covers are heavy-duty LDPE bags manufactured for oversized industrial goods, equipment, machinery and bulk materials that cannot be accommodated by standard bag sizes. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures jumbo polybags and covers in Pimpri MIDC, Pune — supplying engineering companies, automotive manufacturers, logistics operators and industrial packers across Maharashtra and India.

Our jumbo polybags and polythene covers are manufactured in custom dimensions — from extra-large bags for palletised goods to full machine covers several metres wide. Gusseted, flat and tubular formats are available. Thickness ranges from 50 microns to 250+ microns depending on weight-bearing and protection requirements. Custom printing with statutory information and product branding can be added. We are a trusted large plastic bag manufacturer and wholesale supplier for industrial packaging in the Pune–Maharashtra region.

Standard polythene bags cannot accommodate large industrial products such as machine components, steel structures, rolled goods, large castings and oversized packaging. Jumbo polybags are fabricated specifically to these requirements — welded from LDPE sheeting in custom widths from 50 to 100+ inches, any length and with or without gussets.

We supply jumbo polybags from 50 microns (government minimum) to 250+ microns depending on the weight and nature of the contents. Open-mouth and heat-seal top closures are available. Gusseted configurations are also offered for improved fit around irregular shapes.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is the maximum size of jumbo polybags you can manufacture?
We manufacture jumbo polybags in virtually any custom size. Our extrusion lines can produce bags up to 100+ inches wide and any length. For very large machine covers or industrial tarpaulin-style polythene covers, we also offer welded and heat-sealed constructions. Contact us with your dimensions and we will advise on the best construction method and thickness.
What thickness of jumbo polybag is recommended for heavy industrial goods?
For general large goods and machine covers, 100–150 microns is commonly used. For heavier or sharp-edged items, 200–250 microns provides added protection. All bags are manufactured to the government minimum of 50 microns. We advise on the appropriate specification based on your product requirements.
Do you supply jumbo polybags to industrial manufacturers across Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply jumbo polybags in bulk to engineering, manufacturing, construction and export companies across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik and all of Maharashtra. Custom dimensions are our speciality — contact us for a quote.
What industries use jumbo polybags?
Jumbo polybags are used across automotive (for protecting assembled vehicles or large assemblies), engineering (covering machinery during transit or storage), logistics (oversized pallet covers), construction (material storage), pharmaceutical (bulk ingredient storage) and agriculture (covering large harvested produce). Any industry that handles large or irregularly shaped items benefits from custom jumbo polythene covers.
