Product Overview

About LDPE Shrink Film

LDPE shrink film is a heat-shrinkable plastic film made from Low-Density Polyethylene that contracts tightly around products when heat is applied, creating a secure, protective and professional-looking package. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures LDPE shrink film rolls from our Pimpri MIDC, Pune facility — catering to industrial, pharmaceutical and product packaging applications across Maharashtra.

Our LDPE shrink film is ideal for bundling, unitising and protecting industrial goods during transit and storage. When heat is applied using a heat gun or shrink tunnel, the film contracts uniformly to conform tightly to the product surface. We recommend approximately 100 microns thickness for best shrinking properties and reliable performance, though thinner films from 50 microns can also be produced.

LDPE shrink film is supplied in flat rolls in custom widths. It is widely used for pallet top sheeting, industrial bundling, machine wrapping and product protection. Custom roll widths and lengths are available on bulk orders.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What thickness of LDPE shrink film do you recommend?
We can manufacture LDPE shrink film from 50 microns, but recommend approximately 100 microns thickness for reliable shrinking performance and good product protection. The ideal thickness depends on the size and weight of the product being wrapped.
What equipment is needed to use LDPE shrink film?
LDPE shrink film can be used with a heat gun for small-scale manual applications, or with a shrink tunnel for larger volume production lines. We can advise on compatible film widths and thicknesses for your existing equipment and application.
Do you supply LDPE shrink film in bulk to manufacturers in Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply LDPE shrink film in bulk to pharmaceutical, industrial and manufacturing companies across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik and all of Maharashtra.
