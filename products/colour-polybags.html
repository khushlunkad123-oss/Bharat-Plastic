Product Overview

About Any Colour Polybags

Coloured polythene bags serve both functional and branding purposes in industrial and retail packaging. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures LDPE polybags in any colour at our Pimpri MIDC, Pune factory — from standard black, blue and red bags to fully custom colours — supplying bulk and wholesale orders across Maharashtra and India.

Colour coding in plastic bags helps identify products, materials, waste streams and handling instructions in manufacturing and logistics environments. Black LDPE bags are widely used for light-sensitive materials and opaque waste bags. Blue and red bags are common in medical and hazardous material segregation. White bags are used for clean room and food applications.

We produce coloured polybags by incorporating masterbatch into the LDPE during extrusion, ensuring consistent, uniform colour throughout the film. Custom colours are produced by incorporating masterbatch into the LDPE during extrusion, ensuring consistent, uniform colour throughout the film. Custom colour requirements are produced on minimum order quantities.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.
