Product Overview

About LDPE Bags

LDPE bags — low density polyethylene polythene bags — are the most versatile and widely used plastic packaging bags in industrial, commercial and agricultural applications. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. is an ISO 9001:2015-certified LDPE bags manufacturer in Pimpri MIDC, Pune, producing polythene bags in custom sizes, thicknesses and colours for industrial buyers, packaging companies and wholesale distributors across Maharashtra and India since 1970.

Our LDPE polythene bags are manufactured on high-speed extrusion and bag-making lines, allowing us to produce bags from 1-inch width to 100+ inches, in thicknesses from 50 microns (as per government norms) to 500+ microns, and in natural, coloured or printed finishes. LDPE is preferred for polythene bags because of its flexibility, chemical resistance, moisture barrier properties and recyclability. We supply polythene bags in bulk and wholesale quantities to automotive, pharmaceutical, FMCG, agricultural and engineering industries.

Our LDPE bags are produced on modern extrusion and bag-making lines under ISO 9001:2015 certified quality controls. Every batch is checked for dimensional accuracy, thickness consistency and seal strength before dispatch. We manufacture LDPE polythene bags in a full range of standard and custom dimensions — from small component pouches to large industrial liner bags.

As a direct manufacturer, we eliminate middlemen and offer highly competitive factory pricing on all bulk orders. Whether you need plain transparent polythene bags, coloured polythene bags, or custom-printed LDPE bags, we manufacture everything in-house at our Pimpri MIDC, Pune factory.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is the minimum thickness of polythene bags permitted in India?
As per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules, 2021, the minimum thickness for plastic carry bags is 75 microns, and for other plastic packaging (sachets, wrappers etc.) it is 50 microns. All our polythene bags comply with these government regulations. Mandatory statutory printing including thickness, EPR number and manufacturer details is included on all bags.
What printing options are available for LDPE bags?
We offer both flexographic and rotogravure (roto) printing on LDPE bags in multiple colours. Any design — logos, text, product information, regulatory markings, handling instructions — can be printed. Contact us via WhatsApp or the enquiry form with your artwork requirement.
Do you supply LDPE polythene bags in bulk across Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply bulk and wholesale LDPE polythene bags across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik, Aurangabad, Solapur and all of Maharashtra. Pan-India supply is available for larger orders.
What is LDPE and why is it used for polythene bags?
LDPE (Low Density Polyethylene) is a thermoplastic polymer known for its flexibility, toughness, chemical resistance and moisture barrier properties. It is the most widely used material for polythene bags because it is easy to process into thin flexible films, is safe for most packaging applications, and is recyclable. LDPE bags are used across industries from food packaging to industrial parts protection.
