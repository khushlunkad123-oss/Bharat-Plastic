Product Overview

About BOPP Tapes

BOPP packaging tapes are the most widely used carton sealing and box packing tapes in industrial and warehouse environments. Manufactured from biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP) film coated with acrylic or hot-melt adhesive, they provide strong, reliable sealing for cardboard cartons, corrugated boxes and parcels. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures industrial-grade BOPP tapes in Pimpri MIDC, Pune — supplying factories, warehouses, logistics companies and packaging distributors across Maharashtra.

We supply BOPP tapes in clear and brown (buff) variants, in standard 48 mm width and custom widths, with roll lengths of 50 m, 65 m, 100 m and custom. Our tapes are available with acrylic adhesive (water-based, suitable for standard sealing and export packing) and solvent-based adhesive (for high-performance applications). Bulk pricing is available for wholesale buyers, carton box suppliers and packaging companies in Pune and across India.

Our BOPP packaging tapes are produced with a BOPP backing film coated with hot-melt or solvent-based acrylic adhesive for strong, reliable adhesion to cardboard, corrugated boxes and most packaging surfaces. They provide an excellent seal even in high-humidity environments and under varying temperature conditions encountered in logistics and warehouse settings.

We supply BOPP tapes in standard clear and brown (buff). Tape dispensers are available alongside bulk tape orders.

FAQ

Common Questions

What are the standard sizes of BOPP packaging tape?
Standard BOPP packaging tape width is 48 mm (2 inches), with 65 m or 100 m roll lengths. We also manufacture 72 mm (3-inch) wide tapes and custom widths for machine applicators. Custom roll lengths are available for bulk orders. Contact us for wholesale pricing on BOPP tape rolls.
What colours of BOPP tape do you supply?
We supply BOPP packaging tape in standard clear (transparent) and brown (buff) colours in bulk. These are the most commonly used carton sealing tapes in industrial and logistics applications. Contact us for bulk pricing and quantities.
Do you supply BOPP tapes in bulk to factories and warehouses in Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply BOPP packaging tapes in bulk to manufacturers, warehouses, exporters and distributors across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik and all of Maharashtra.
What is the difference between acrylic and solvent-based BOPP tape?
Acrylic BOPP tape uses a water-based acrylic adhesive — it is the most common type, suitable for normal carton sealing, export packaging and general warehousing. Solvent-based BOPP tape uses a hot-melt or solvent-based adhesive for stronger initial tack and better performance in cold or humid environments. We supply both types — contact us to specify your application for the right recommendation.
