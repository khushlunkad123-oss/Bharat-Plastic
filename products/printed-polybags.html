Product Overview

About Printed Polybags

Printed polybags are custom-printed LDPE or BOPP polythene bags that carry your brand's logo, product name, contact details, statutory information and design in one or more colours. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures custom printed polybags in Pimpri MIDC, Pune using flexographic and rotogravure printing — serving retail brands, FMCG companies, e-commerce businesses and industrial manufacturers across Maharashtra and India.

Printed packaging bags are a powerful branding tool — every bag that leaves your facility or store is a moving advertisement for your brand. We offer printing in 1 to 6+ colours using food-safe inks on LDPE polythene bags, carry bags, kirana bags, zip lock bags and BOPP bags. Minimum order quantities vary by bag size and number of print colours. Our design team can help with artwork layout and statutory print requirements. Short lead times and competitive pricing for bulk printed polybag orders.

We offer both flexographic and rotogravure (roto) printing in multiple colours on LDPE polythene bags, tubing and rolls. Any design can be printed — company logos, product names, statutory information, handling instructions, barcodes, regulatory markings, promotional messages and more. Print registration is precise and consistent across the full production run.

Printed polybags are used by garment manufacturers, FMCG brands, food processors, hardware distributors, industrial companies and retailers for branded, informative packaging. We work with your print-ready artwork files to deliver a clean, accurate print result.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What artwork format is required for printed polybags?
We accept print-ready artwork in AI, PDF, EPS or high-resolution JPEG/PNG format. Our design team can assist with artwork preparation if needed. Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 9860256222 to discuss your print requirement.
What is the minimum order quantity for printed polybags?
MOQ for printed polybags varies by size, number of colours and bag specifications. There is an initial plate-making cost for new designs. Contact us for specific MOQ and pricing for your requirement.
How long does it take to produce printed polybags?
Lead time for printed polybags is typically 7–15 working days from artwork approval and order confirmation, depending on quantity and complexity. Contact us for a specific timeline for your requirement.
How many colours can be printed on polybags?
We offer flexographic printing in 1 to 6 colours on polythene bags. Single-colour printing (typically black or a spot colour) is the most economical. Multi-colour printing allows full brand reproduction including logos, gradients and product images. Rotogravure printing is available for very high-volume orders with the highest print quality. Contact us to discuss your design and colour requirements.
What is the minimum order quantity for printed polybags?
Minimum order quantities for printed polybags depend on the bag size, number of colours and type of printing. Generally, MOQs start from 10,000–50,000 bags for single-colour flexographic printing, with higher MOQs for multi-colour designs. Contact us with your bag specifications and artwork and we will advise on the most cost-effective order quantity and pricing.
