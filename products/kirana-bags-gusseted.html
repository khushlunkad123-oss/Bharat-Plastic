About Kirana Bags

Kirana bags — also called gusseted polythene bags or carry bags — are LDPE bags with side gussets that expand to carry groceries, produce and retail goods. They are the standard packaging bag used by kirana stores, supermarkets, vegetable vendors and retailers across India. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures kirana bags and gusseted polythene bags in Pimpri MIDC, Pune — supplying retail shops, FMCG distributors and packaging wholesalers across Maharashtra.

Gusseted bags differ from flat polybags in that their side or bottom gussets allow the bag to expand and stand upright when filled, making them practical for carrying groceries and produce. Our kirana bags are available in seven standard sizes (from small to large) as well as custom dimensions, in natural (milky-white), coloured and printed variants. All bags are manufactured at minimum 50 microns thickness in compliance with India's Plastic Waste Management Rules, and carry mandatory statutory printing. Bulk and wholesale supply is available.

What is the difference between flat polybags and gusseted kirana bags?
Flat polybags are sealed on three sides and open at the top — they lie flat when empty. Gusseted bags have side or bottom folds (gussets) that unfold when the bag is filled, allowing it to expand, stand upright and carry more volume. Gusseted kirana bags are the preferred carry bags for retail and grocery use because they hold more and are easier for customers to carry.
Do your kirana bags comply with India's plastic bag regulations?
Yes. All our kirana bags and carry bags are manufactured at a minimum thickness of 50 microns as required by the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules, 2021.
