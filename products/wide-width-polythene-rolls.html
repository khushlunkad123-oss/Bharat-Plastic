Product Overview

About Wide-Width Polythene Rolls

Wide-width polythene rolls are large-format LDPE plastic films used for covering large surface areas in construction, agriculture, civil engineering and industrial applications. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures wide-width polythene rolls at our Pimpri MIDC, Pune facility — available in widths from several feet to over 15 feet, and in any required length and thickness.

Standard polythene bags and rolls are not suitable for large-area industrial applications. Wide-width rolls solve this by providing a single continuous sheet wide enough to cover large surface areas without joins, reducing installation time and improving performance.

We supply wide-width polythene in natural/clear, black (for UV protection) and custom colours. Custom widths and lengths are produced to order for industrial requirements.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is the widest polythene roll you can manufacture?
We can manufacture polythene rolls over 200 inches (5+ metres) wide at our Pimpri MIDC, Pune factory using our wide-format extrusion lines. Custom widths are produced to order — contact us with your project requirement.
What thickness of wide-width polythene do you recommend for industrial use?
For general industrial surface protection, 50–100 microns is typical. For heavier applications, 150–250 microns is recommended. All thicknesses above the government minimum of 50 microns are available — contact us to discuss your specific requirement.
Do you supply wide-width polythene rolls to industrial buyers in Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply wide-width LDPE polythene rolls to manufacturers, industrial facilities, construction companies and pharmaceutical buyers across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik and all of Maharashtra.
