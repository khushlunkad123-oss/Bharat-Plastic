Product Overview

About PP Bags

PP bags — polypropylene packing bags — are high-clarity transparent plastic bags manufactured from polypropylene film. Compared to LDPE bags, PP bags offer superior optical clarity, higher stiffness, better gloss and a crisper, more premium appearance — making them the preferred packaging for garments, hardware, stationery, toys and retail products where maximum product visibility matters. Bharat Plastic Manufacturing Co. manufactures PP bags in Pimpri MIDC, Pune, supplying retail brands, garment exporters and packaging companies across Maharashtra and India.

Our polypropylene bags are available in plain transparent form as well as with self-adhesive flap (also called BOPP self-adhesive bags), with side gussets for fuller items, and with header cards for hanging displays. Widths range from 2 inches to 24+ inches, with custom lengths. Thickness options from 35 microns (ultra-clear display bags) to 100+ microns (heavy-duty PP bags). We supply PP bags wholesale in standard and custom sizes to garment brands, exporters and retail packaging distributors.

PP bags are widely used for packaging agricultural produce such as leaves, herbs and fresh produce, where high transparency and some stiffness are desirable. They are also popular for garment packing, textile accessories, stationery items, gift packaging and other retail applications where product visibility is important.

We supply PP bags in a range of standard and custom sizes. The bags feature open-mouth and heat-seal closure options. All bags are manufactured to the minimum 50 micron thickness as per government norms and carry mandatory statutory printing.

Regulatory Compliance

All products carry mandatory statutory printing as per Government of India norms — including EPR number, manufacturer name & address, product category, thickness and all other required information. Minimum thickness is 50 microns as per the Plastic Waste Management (Amendment) Rules. Our EPR registration ensures full compliance with extended producer responsibility requirements.

FAQ

Common Questions

What are the advantages of PP bags over LDPE bags?
PP (polypropylene) bags have higher clarity and gloss than LDPE bags, giving packaged products a more premium look. PP bags are also stiffer and crinkle less, maintaining their shape better on retail shelves or hangers. LDPE bags are softer, more flexible and better suited for industrial and grocery packaging. For retail, garment and gift packaging, PP bags are generally preferred for their appearance.
Can PP bags be printed with a logo or brand name?
Yes. Polypropylene bags can be printed with flexographic or screen printing in one to multiple colours. Custom logo, brand name, barcode or product information can be printed on PP bags. Printed PP bags are popular for garment brands, retail boutiques and gift packaging. Contact us with your artwork and quantity to get a printing quote.
Do you supply PP bags in bulk to buyers in Maharashtra?
Yes. We supply transparent PP bags in bulk to agricultural, retail, garment and industrial buyers across Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Nashik, Aurangabad and all of Maharashtra.
